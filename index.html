What is Expense Tracker?

Personal finance manager with role‑based categories: Student, Business, Other. Track tuition, rent, inventory, bills, and more.

Features

  • 👩‍🎓 Student mode: tuition, books, hostel
  • 💼 Business mode: salary, inventory, tax
  • 🏦 Multiple bank accounts
  • 📊 Charts with % breakdown
  • 🔐 2FA & Password change
  • 🔍 Search transactions
  • 📁 Export CSV

How It Works

Choose your role — Student, Business, or Other during registration. Categories & suggestions adapt automatically.
Link banks — Add HBL, UBL, etc. Track net worth in real time.
Smart shopping list — Add role‑specific items (e.g., "Tuition Fee - 50000") and convert to expense in one click.

Security

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and encrypted local storage. Your data stays private.
